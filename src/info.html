About SkibiOS

SkibiOS is an operating system completely based on the concept of universal apps. The idea stemmed from not being able to use most of my steam games when I made the switch to mac. It then sparked an idea for me to create my own operating system, for anyone. No matter if you are an apple user, a Raspberry Pi user, a poweruser, SkibiOS is for you.
The name SkibiOS was formed from the song "scatman" from Scatman John. Ski-bap-bap bada-bop. Bap-bap bada-bop. Ski...biOS! And that was the dumb reasoning behind the name SkibiOS.
